Our friendship started in the place that was a little different from the others...the basketball court. He will tell me what I don't wanna hear, even if I'm not equipped to hear or address it.

I tribute this page to him because not only has been a friend, but he has been family. I've been very receptive of information with him. He's one of the few people who I can have a hoops conversation with and not be annoyed. We talk ball & sports all day for real. He's just a cool dude to kick it with. He's also been my gaming plug for YEARS. I can still remember introducing him to my retro console, & I almost thought I wasn't gonna get it back, but we have been able to hang and be there for each other every step of the way, & that's what people need in the end.
